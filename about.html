Discover AQUAFinder: Your Campus Hydration Guide

AQUAFinder is a user-friendly web application designed to help students and visitors locate water fountains across the University of Reading Campus. With an interactive map and detailed fountain information for fountain across campus, staying hydrated has never been easier.

Our platform offers the following features:

  • Interactive map with markers for buildings containing water fountains
  • Detailed fountain information, including location and functionality

If you find a fountain that hasn't been added to the website, please contribute and help us expand our database. If you encounter a broken or inaccessible fountain, click the report button to let us know.
